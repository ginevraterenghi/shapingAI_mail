Shaping 21st Century AI is a 3-year research project funded by the ESRC as part of the Open Research Area scheme involving a consortium of research teams from four countries (Germany, France, Canada, and the UK). The project investigates Artificial Intelligence (AI) as a socio-technical phenomenon: AI is not only an innovative scientific methodology and technology that is being introduced into our societies, it also entails a distinctive approach to solving social problems through automation and potentially new ways of organising society and the public sphere.
